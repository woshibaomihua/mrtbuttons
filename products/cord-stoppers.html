Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between pig-nose, mushroom and round cord locks?

Pig-nose locks usually have a wider oval face and are popular on hoodies and bags. Mushroom locks have a flatter button-like top and can feel more compact. Round or cylindrical cord locks are common for clean functional drawcord adjustment on jackets and outdoor wear.

Can you recommend a model based on our cord diameter?

Yes. Send the cord diameter, finished product photo or tech pack and we can suggest suitable models from the sheets above. Most boards already indicate the supported hole diameter, which makes style screening faster.

Do you offer custom colors and white versions?

Yes. Black is the most common stock color, but white POM and custom colored production are available subject to MOQ and material choice.

Can you assemble the cord stopper together with cord and end tips?

Yes. We can quote assembled programs that combine the stopper, braided cord and cord-end finishing, similar to the assembled sample image shown on this page.

What information should I send for a cord stopper quote?

Please send the preferred model or a marked image from this page, cord diameter, color, material preference, quantity and whether you need loose parts or assembled drawcord units.
