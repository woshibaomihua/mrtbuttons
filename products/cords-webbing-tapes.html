We supply cords and narrow-fabric trims for apparel and accessory factories, including flat shoelaces, round shoelaces, cotton rope, PP hollow drawcord, reflective rope, latex elastic cord and spooled elastic programs.

For B2B orders, we match the cord construction to the use case: soft cotton rope for packaging and fashion, PP or polyester drawcord for garments and bags, reflective rope for outdoor products, and elastic cord for craft, garment or fastening applications.

Frequently asked questions

Can you custom-match cord color and diameter?

Yes. We support stock black/white items as well as custom colors and diameter development for shoelaces, braided cords and elastic cords, subject to MOQ and construction.

Can you supply cut-to-length cords with tips?

Yes. We can quote bulk-by-meter supply or finished cut lengths with plastic or metal aglets, heat-cut ends or other finishing based on the end use.

Do you also offer webbing, hook-and-loop and elastic band?

Yes. Webbing straps, hook-and-loop tape, elastic band and ribbon tape can be quoted from samples, artwork or technical drawings.
