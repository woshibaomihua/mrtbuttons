Frequently asked questions

Which zipper type should I choose for my product?

Metal zippers are best for denim, workwear, jackets and bags; nylon coil is flexible and lightweight for garments and pouches; invisible zippers suit dresses and skirts; resin zippers are preferred for sporty or chunky-tooth looks.

Can you customize slider logos and puller shapes?

Yes. We can develop custom pullers, engraved slider logos and matching finish options for OEM/ODM zipper projects.

Do you supply zipper rolls as well as finished lengths?

Yes. We supply both continuous zipper chain / rolls and finished zippers in open-end, close-end and two-way constructions.

Can you match our zipper tape color?

Yes. Provide Pantone references, fabric swatches or a physical sample and we can match the zipper tape color for production.

What information should I send for a zipper quote?

Please send zipper type, size, length, open-end or close-end construction, tape color, tooth color, slider style, quantity and garment or bag application. A photo or physical sample is very helpful.
