Frequently asked questions

Can you match hardware accessories to our strap width?

Yes. Send the strap or webbing width, thickness, end use and preferred finish, and we can recommend compatible buckles, rings, hooks or slide adjusters.

What finishes are available for metal hardware accessories?

Common finishes include nickel, shiny gold, light gold, rose gold, gunmetal, black, antique brass and antique silver. Custom plating can be discussed for bulk orders.

Can you supply hooks, rings and buckles as a matched set?

Yes. We can quote matched hardware sets for bags, leashes, belts, lanyards and garment straps, including hooks, D-rings, O-rings, slides and connectors.

Can these hardware accessories be customized?

For suitable styles, we can discuss custom finish, logo marking, adjusted size, special plating and new tooling based on your sample, drawing or technical specification.

What information is needed for a quote?

Please send the reference image, target size or strap width, material and finish, quantity, application and whether you need individual hardware or a complete matched set.
