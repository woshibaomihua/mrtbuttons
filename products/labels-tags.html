Frequently asked questions

Can you make our own logo in a similar metal label style?

Yes. These uploaded examples can be used as a reference direction, and we can discuss your own logo file, shape, finish, attachment method and dimensions for OEM development.

What finishes are commonly available for metal labels and tags?

Common finishes include shiny gold, matte gold, silver, gunmetal, black, antique brass, rose gold and other plated tones, depending on the item construction.

How can the labels be attached to the product?

Attachment methods vary by design and may include sewing holes, side tabs, fold-in legs, hanging loops, rivet-ready holes or other custom mounting structures.
