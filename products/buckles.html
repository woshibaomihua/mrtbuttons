Frequently asked questions

Can you match buckle size to our webbing width?

Yes. Share the target strap or webbing width and we can recommend the closest buckle, ladder lock, triglide or hook size before sampling.

Do you supply matching buckle sets and companion components?

Yes. We can quote coordinated sets including side-release buckles, adjusters, triglides, D-rings, swivel hooks and other strap hardware according to your project.

Are black buckles available faster than custom colors?

Usually yes. Black is the most common stock color for side-release buckles, adjusters and hooks. Custom colors can be matched subject to quantity and lead time.
