We manufacture the full range of decorative fashion buttons: pearl and rhinestone statement buttons, crystal and gemstone-look designs, enamel styles, floral shank buttons, sunburst fashion buttons and soft fabric-covered buttons. Every style is made to your design — shape, size, color, plating (gold, silver, gunmetal), pearl tone and stone setting — with a digital proof and a physical sample before bulk. Nickel-free plating, custom shanks and skin-safe materials are available for direct-contact and children’s garments.

Frequently asked questions

What decorative button types can you make?

Pearl, rhinestone, crystal, gemstone-look, enamel and fabric-covered buttons, plus geometric and shaped designs — in shank, sew-through or rhinestone-claw settings.

Are the rhinestones securely set?

Stones are claw-set or fixed with industrial adhesive and pull-tested. We advise the best setting for your garment and washing method.

Can you match a designer button I send?

Yes. Send a sample or a clear photo and we quote a close match or a custom mold, with a physical sample approved before bulk.

Do you offer nickel-free plating for skin contact?

Yes — nickel-free and OEKO-TEX® compliant materials are available for womenswear and children's garments, with SGS/Intertek testing on request.
