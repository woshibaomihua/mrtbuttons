Natural buttons bring warmth, texture and material character that synthetic buttons cannot easily replicate. Merit Trims supplies mother-of-pearl shell buttons, corozo fruit buttons, genuine horn buttons, wood buttons, wooden toggle closures and coconut shell buttons for shirts, knitwear, tailoring, outerwear, resortwear and craft collections.

Corozo buttons (tagua nut, “vegetable ivory”) are valued for clean machining, full size runs and rich dyeing results. Genuine horn buttons offer natural smoky, tortoise and dark polished tones for premium suiting and coats. Wood buttons cover unfinished maple-look buttons, stained dark wood buttons, painted kids buttons, development boards and toggle closures. Coconut shell buttons add rustic two-tone shell texture, engraved patterns, natural rings and slider buckles for linen, resortwear, bags and accessories.

Frequently asked questions

Are mother-of-pearl shell buttons suitable for premium shirts?

Yes. Mother-of-pearl shell buttons are widely used for premium shirts, blouses and resortwear because of their natural luster, cool touch and refined appearance. We can match size, thickness, hole layout and color tone to your sample.

Are corozo buttons really more sustainable than plastic?

Corozo is the dried seed of the tagua palm — a renewable crop harvested without felling trees, and the finished button is biodegradable. It is widely used by brands to replace polyester buttons in sustainability programs.

Can horn buttons be identical in color?

No two horn buttons are identical — variegation is the material value. We grade and batch-match so variation stays within an agreed range; ask for a graded sample card.

What sizes are common for genuine horn suit buttons?

For jackets and suits, common horn size runs include 44L and 40L for fronts, with 32L, 28L or 24L for sleeves. We can match full size sets, thickness and edge style based on your garment specification.

How should garments with natural shell buttons be washed?

Shell and corozo buttons handle normal garment finishing well when matched to the right thickness and coating. For premium garments, avoid prolonged soaking, strong bleaching and heavy tumble abrasion; we can advise testing based on your care label.

Can you engrave or emboss our logo on natural buttons?

Yes. Laser engraving, embossing and mold branding are available depending on material, logo detail and order quantity. Shell, corozo and wood are suitable for premium custom branding.

Do you supply real horn buttons in full size runs?

Yes. We can supply coordinated real horn size runs such as 24L, 28L, 32L, 36L, 40L and 44L for jacket fronts and sleeves, with matched color families like black, coffee, smoky or tortoise.

Can you match sample-board colors for genuine horn buttons?

Yes. The uploaded horn size boards are useful for style confirmation and color-family matching. Because horn is a natural material, we batch-match within an agreed tolerance and can send approval samples before bulk production.

What are corozo fruit buttons?

Corozo fruit buttons are made from tagua nut, also called vegetable ivory. They are natural, machine well, take dye cleanly and are widely used for premium shirts, jackets, knitwear and sustainability-focused apparel programs.

Can you make corozo sample boards with full size runs?

Yes. We can build sample boards by style number, size and color, including 16L–40L and 24L–40L runs such as GS-6032 to GS-6048, with ivory, khaki, coffee, dark grey, black-blue and black references.

Which small corozo button sizes are available for shirts?

Common small stock sizes include 16L, 18L and 20L, especially for shirts, cuffs, childrenswear and lightweight garments. Style ranges such as GS-6012 to GS-6023 can be quoted by color and quantity.

Can you supply corozo buttons by style number such as GS-6046 or GS-6012?

Yes. Send the style number, size, color and quantity. We can quote based on the sample-board code, or match from a photo if the exact code needs to be confirmed.

Do you offer wooden toggles for duffle coats and knitwear?

Yes. We supply wooden toggle buttons in multiple shapes and colors, plus complete toggle closures with rope loops and stitched faux-leather tabs for coats, capes, knitwear and bags.

Can you make painted wood buttons in custom colors?

Yes. For bulk orders we can produce painted wooden buttons in standard garment colors or custom Pantone-style references, subject to practical finish tolerance and base wood selection.

What styles of coconut shell buttons do you supply?

We supply dark and light coconut shell buttons, 2-hole and 4-hole styles, laser-engraved decorative buttons, stitch-effect coconut buttons, coconut shell rings and coconut slider buckles.

Are coconut shell buttons suitable for resortwear and linen garments?

Yes. Coconut shell buttons are a strong fit for resortwear, linen, beachwear, handmade garments and eco-inspired collections because they provide a rustic natural texture with lightweight comfort.
